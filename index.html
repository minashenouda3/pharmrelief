Platform Features

Everything relief staffing needs to run smoothly

From shift matching to mileage tracking and digital contracts — PharmRelief handles the operations so you can focus on patient care.

Every pharmacist on the platform is verified against the Ontario College of Pharmacists registry before they can accept a shift.

Double-Confirm Shift Flow

Shifts only lock once both the pharmacy and pharmacist confirm — eliminating no-shows and last-minute confusion.

Digital Contracts & Compliance

Training certificates, digital agreements, and compliance documents — collected automatically before a shift begins.
