🚀 Go live — complete hosting guide

How to deploy PharmRelief so pharmacies and pharmacists can access it today. Three paths from fastest to most robust.

Option 1 — Netlify drag-and-drop Fastest · 5 minutes · Free
Best for: getting something live today for pilot pharmacies to test
Step 1 — Go to netlify.com
Create a free account. No credit card needed. Go to app.netlify.com
Step 2 — Drag and drop this HTML file
On the Netlify dashboard you will see a box that says "Drag and drop your site folder here". Drag the pharmrelief_complete.html file directly into that box.
Step 3 — You are live in 30 seconds
Netlify generates a URL like amazing-name-abc123.netlify.app
Share that URL with pilot pharmacies immediately. They can open it on any device.
Step 4 — Custom domain (optional, $12/yr)
Buy pharmrelief.ca from Namecheap ($12/yr). In Netlify: Settings → Domain management → Add custom domain. Point your domain's DNS to Netlify. Done — HTTPS is automatic.
